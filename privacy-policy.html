Privacy Policy for Flowdoku

Last updated: 3 March 2026

Flowdoku ("the app") is a sudoku puzzle game developed as a personal project. This policy explains what information is collected when you use the app and how it is used.

Information we collect

The app itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no user accounts, no registration, and no data entered by you leaves your device through our code.

Third-party advertising

The app displays banner advertisements served by third-party advertising networks. These networks may automatically collect certain information from your device in order to serve relevant ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location derived from it
  • Device type, operating system, and language settings
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by the advertising network under their own privacy policies. We do not receive or store this data ourselves. You can typically opt out of personalised advertising through your device settings (Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads).

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
